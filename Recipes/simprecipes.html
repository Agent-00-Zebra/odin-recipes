Breakfast Cereal

Description

Cereal, formally termed breakfast cereal, is a traditional breakfast food made from processed cereal grains. It is traditionally eaten as part of breakfast, or a snack food, primarily in Western societies.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal of your choosing

Instructions

  1. Pour cereal into an empty bowl. (Very important to do this first)
  2. Add milk to the bowl (Don't drown the cereal in milk.)
  3. Grab a spoon and enjoy!

Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves—jelly—spread on bread. The sandwich may be open-faced, made of a single slice of bread folded over, or made between two slices of bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread (of your choice)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Instructions

  1. Grab one slice of bread and spread a thin layer of peanut butter on the bread.
  2. Grab the other slice of bread and spread a thin layer of jelly on the bread.
  3. Put both slices of bread together (with the peanut butter and jelly facing each other).
  4. Enjoy!

Yogurt Parfait

Description

Yogurt mixed with fruit, granola and/or nuts.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt
  • Fruit
  • Granola
  • Nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add yogurt to a bowl
  2. Add fruit to the bowl
  3. Add granola (and nuts if you want)
  4. Grab a spoon and mix the ingredients together.
  5. Enjoy!
