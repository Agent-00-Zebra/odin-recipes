Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a dish made from eggs stirred, whipped or beaten together while being gently heated, typically with salt, butter, oil and sometimes other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 1-2 Tbsp Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat pan on medium heat
  2. Crack 2-3 eggs in a bowl (No shells)
  3. Add 1-2 tbps of butter and oil to pan
  4. Add a sprinkle of salt and pepper to eggs in bowl
  5. Whisk eggs until they're uniform.
  6. Whisk eggs into pan
  7. When eggs start to turn into a bright yellow begin scrambling periodically.
  8. Turn off heat and plate eggs when there is no longer liquid in the pan.
  9. Enjoy!

French Toast

Description

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried. Alternative names and variants include "eggy bread", "Bombay toast", "gypsy toast", and "poor knights".

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of Bread (of your choice)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 cup MIlk
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Heat pan on medium heat
  2. Crack 2 eggs in a bowl/container that can fit your bread laying down
  3. Add 2/3 cups of milk to the bowl/container and whisk together
  4. Add a drop or two of Vanilla Extract and mix it in.
  5. Sprinkle Cinnamon and Sugar into the mixture.
  6. Add 1-2 tbps of butter to pan
  7. Dip bread in bowl/container make sure to fully soak both sides in the mixture
  8. Put bread in the pan and cook for about 3-5 min for each side
  9. Plate and add either syrup or powdered sugar (if you like)
  10. Enjoy!

Orange Chicken

Description

Orange chicken is an American Chinese dish.

Main Ingredient

  • 2 pounds of chicken thighs

Batter Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 cup of cornstarch
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tbsp of oil

Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of orange juice
  • 1/4 cup of vinegar
  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp of water and cornstarch mixed

Instructions

  1. Cut 2 pounds of chicken thighs into about 1 inch pieces.
  2. Add them to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and cornstarch into a bowl
  4. Whisk the flour into the bowl previously mentioned
  5. Add egg into the bowl
  6. Slowly pour and whisk in the water to make the batter for the chicken
  7. Mix chicken into the batter and let it chill in the fridge for at least 30 min
  8. Fill pot/pan with oil enough to cover the pieces of chicken
  9. Turn the heat to high
  10. Cook chicken and take out when it begins to float.
  11. Grab another pan and put on medium heat.
  12. Add oil, chili flakes, ginger, garlic, sugar, and orange juice then bring it to a light boil.
  13. Mix in vinegar and bring to a light boil.
  14. Mix on spy sauce and bring to a light boil.
  15. Slowly mix in water and cornstarch mixture.
  16. Mix cooked chicken into the sauce.
  17. Plate & Enjoy!
